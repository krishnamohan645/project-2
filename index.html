How Travel Agency work

In simple terms, a travel agency helps people plan and book their trips. They assist with things like finding flights, booking hotels, organizing activities, and making sure travelers have the necessary documents. Travel agencies make the process easier by offering advice, handling bookings, and providing support throughout the journey.
